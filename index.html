SFPD Hierachy Visualizations

About these visualizations

As part of the data visualization course, we were asked to visualize this dataset, sfpd, as a hierarchical data set.
However, the dataset is not hierarchical. We had to decide how to form a hierarchy for this dataset. Details are in the data page.
For these particular visualizations I used the city of San Francisco as a root, the batallions as the following level, and finally the SFFD stations associated to each batallion as the final level.
